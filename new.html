About Us

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore campus, conferred degrees upon a total of 2077 students during its 20th convocation ceremony. Among these graduates, 750 were female and 1327 were male The majority of students, 1120 in number, were from the B.Tech stream, followed by M.Tech (326), MBA (132), Integrated MSc. (121), B.Sc (Hon) Agriculture (117), MSc. (72), and B.A (44) programs.

Around 94% of eligible Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore students got placed this year for B.Tech CBE and 65% for M.Tech CBE campus. The average annual salary for students passing out batch 2023 as on date is 9.6 LPA for BTech and 8.96 LPA for MTech. The highest salary offered for 2023 batch as on date is 56 LPA.
